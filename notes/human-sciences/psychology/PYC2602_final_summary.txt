## UNIT 2: Prenatal Development
### Orientation
- Human development starts from conception to birth (approximately 280 days).
- Focus on genetic and environmental factors affecting development.

### Key Points
- Genetic foundations shape both commonalities and uniqueness in individuals (phenotypes vs. genotypes).
- Prenatal factors can hinder or enhance development:
  - Character traits are established during prenatal phase.
  - Environmental exposure before birth plays a critical role.
  - Substantial growth occurs in this period compared to later life stages.
  - Attitudes of parents and significant others are formed, influencing postnatal interactions.
